Lentil Bread

Simple high-fiber lentil bread made entirely from soaked lentils. Great as a dense toast or bread replacement.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dry lentils
  • Water for soaking
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1–2 tablespoons olive oil (optional)
  • Seasonings to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak lentils 4 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  3. Blend lentils with salt, baking powder, and oil.
  4. Pour into loaf pan.
  5. Bake 40–45 minutes.
  6. Cool fully before slicing.

Notes

  • Seasonings are optional and flexible.
  • Best texture when cooled completely.
