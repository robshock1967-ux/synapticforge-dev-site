Lentil Bread

Simple high-fiber lentil bread made entirely from soaked lentils. Great as a dense toast or bread replacement.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dry lentils
  • Water for soaking
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1–2 tablespoons olive oil (optional)
  • Seasonings to taste (garlic powder, onion powder, herbs, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Soak lentils 4 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain lentils. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  3. Blend lentils with salt, baking powder, and oil until smooth.
  4. Pour into loaf pan lined with parchment.
  5. Bake 40–45 minutes until set.
  6. Cool completely before slicing for best texture.

Notes

  • Seasonings are optional and flexible.
  • Texture improves when fully cooled.
